Going total wireless is a current trend today. With the evolution of laptops, smart phones and other hand held devices, wired system has become an obsolete technology. With new 802.11ac standards, now we can achieve near wired speed of 1 Gigabit per second through wireless systems.

Internet has become lifeline of today's world, everyone needs to be connected 24x7, wired system brings serious limitation, whether siting in coffee house or travelling through metro, all of us want to be connected and able to access internet.

Health Care

Hospitals are increasingly going digital, all reports, case history, day to day procedures are increasingly captured in central repository and are available to doctors in their hand held devices. Wireless system acts as a back bone to the complete infrastructure.

Tele Medicine

Another area that is increasingly gaining traction, today telemedicine and virtual doctors are gaining popularity, these not only provides access to range of specialist but also gives near real time consultation experience. Since the system should be maneuvered and increasing mobile, wireless system acts as a backbone to the complete setup.


Education Campus

Student needs to connected, whether to access the lectures or surf the internet, watch presentation or collaborate, life in campus cannot be imagined without wireless. Students want to access the network while in class, ground, canteen or hostel, wireless system acts as backbone to the complete setup.

Ware House

Imagine a large warehouse and you need to take inventory, one cannot imagine work with wireless bar code scanners. The wireless system again acts a back bone to the complete infrastructure.

Corporate Houses

Enterprises need agility, the work force has be increasing mobile, each individual today is carrying at least 2 devices that needs to be connected wirelessly, with smart workspaces there is no room of wires.

Homes & Residential

With growth in IT sector, every individual wants the same facility or user experience provided in office to be available at home, with number of devices, it is very tough to have wires and connection everywhere, wireless system act as the back bone to connect all the devices.

Smart Connected Cites

The concept of smart cites is built on strong wireless foundation. A smart city can be defined as the one having smart mobility, smart utilities, smart governance, smart real estate, smart health care and smart traffic and monitoring system. It is taking the digitalization to the next level.
